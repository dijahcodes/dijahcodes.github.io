About Me

Welcome to my programming portfolio website! I'm Dijah, a full-stack developer with a passion for accessibility, travel, and learning languages. I bring websites to life with React (and a lot of coffee!). As a first generation, Black college student with a dash of ADHD, I believe that technology should be inclusive and accessible to all, and I strive to create solutions that cater to diverse user needs.

Beyond working with React, I am also deeply committed to expanding my knowledge and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. Currently, I am actively exploring the intriguing realm of cybersecurity, aiming to enhance my skills and contribute to creating secure digital environments.
